Required Documents Checklist

Please prepare the following documents. You may be asked to present them during the admission process.

  • TC (Transfer Certificate)
  • Marksheet
  • Migration (for other boards)
  • Birth Certificate
  • Aadhar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Samagra ID
  • Caste Certificate
  • MPTAAS (For SC/ST only)
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Passbook
  • Parent's ID
